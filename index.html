Accessibility Statement for Flecha Web Design

Our Commitment

At Flecha Web Design, we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for everyone, including people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure our digital products are inclusive.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Flecha Web Design is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not yet fully conform to the accessibility standard (for example, we may be in the process of adding descriptive alt-text to older portfolio pieces).

Accessibility Features

To ensure a seamless experience, our website currently includes:

Keyboard Navigation: All essential site functions are accessible via keyboard.

Screen Reader Compatibility: Proper use of ARIA labels and semantic HTML.

Contrast Ratios: Text and UI elements meet or exceed minimum contrast requirements for readability.

Responsive Design: Content scales gracefully on all devices and zoom levels up to 400%.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Flecha Web Design. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on our site.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing journey. We perform regular audits and updates to our site to ensure we remain compliant with 2026 industry standards.
