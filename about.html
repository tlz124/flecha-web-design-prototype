About the Name

Why we chose the arrow

The name "Flecha Web Design" holds special meaning for us. "Flecha" is the Spanish word for "arrow," symbolizing direction, precision, and forward movement. Just as an arrow points toward a target with purpose and clarity, we help businesses and educational institutions navigate the digital landscape with confidence.

We chose this name because we believe great web design should do more than look good—it should guide users exactly where they need to go and help organizations move toward their goals. Every arrow has a direction, and we're here to make sure yours is pointed toward success.
